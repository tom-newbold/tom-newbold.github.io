BeGreen (ECM2434 Group Project)

BeGreen is a django-powered web-based social media app, designed to encorage sustainability on campus through the use of "eco-challenges". Users are encoraged to submit photos of them completing the sustainable activity, and to interact with submissions of their friends and others on the app. The full repository is available here, where the project README can be found, along with other related resources.
